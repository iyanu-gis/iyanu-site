Up until 2016, about 160 blue plaques have been on many of Leeds' historical buildings to celebrate and remember notable people and events.

This webpage shows 107 markers representing blue plaques installed across Leeds, West Yorkshire, England. Each marker shows the plaque's location, unveiler, date unveiled, and sponsor with attached images.
