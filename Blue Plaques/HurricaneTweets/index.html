Global map showing tweets from the 2012 Hurricane Sandy

Hurricanes are unfortunate and destructive natural disasters that occur between the months of June and November affecting countries bordered by the Atlantic Ocean. On the 22nd October 2012, Hurricane Sandy, termed as the most destructive hurricane of the year started on the coasts of Jamaica. The Hurricane spanned through the Caribbean and Bermuda, United States, and Canada, destroying over $70.4 Billion and killing 147 people1.

Social media has always played an important role in information sharing, and its significance was highly felt during the period of the hurricane as information was obtained from tweets on Twitter including other social media platforms2.
